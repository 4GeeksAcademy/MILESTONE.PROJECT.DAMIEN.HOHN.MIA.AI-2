Two departments whose problems stand out most are Patient Experience and Access and Technology. The Patient Experience team is dealing with a 22% no-show rate and no unified booking system, which directly impacts both patient care and company revenue. The Technology department is also critical because all other departments depend on fixing the disconnected systems, lack of shared data, and missing monitoring infrastructure.
The automation challenge I am most interested in is building a no-show prediction and intelligent appointment reminder system for Patient Experience and Access. An AI agent could analyze appointment history, booking patterns, patient communication preferences, clinic location data, and past attendance behavior to predict which appointments are at high risk of being missed. The system could then automatically trigger SMS, email, or app reminders, offer quick rescheduling options, and alert staff when proactive outreach is needed. This would improve patient attendance, reduce lost revenue, and create a smoother patient experience across both the US and UK clinics.


I chose HealthCore because it combines healthcare, AI, and real-world operational challenges in a way that has a direct impact on patients and staff. The company’s problems involve improving efficiency, reducing missed appointments, and connecting outdated systems, which makes it a strong environment for building meaningful AI-powered solutions. I also like that the work involves both technical problem-solving and improving the overall patient experience.
